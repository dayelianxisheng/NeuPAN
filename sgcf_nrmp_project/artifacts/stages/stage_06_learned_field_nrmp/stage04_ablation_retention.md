# Stage-04 Ablation Retention

Stage 04 is retained unchanged as `Learned Geometry Ablation`.

- compare learned and exact observable-clearance accuracy;
- compare model autograd and exact gradients;
- demonstrate feasibility of a learned footprint-clearance proxy;
- explain the evidence-based final architecture choice;
