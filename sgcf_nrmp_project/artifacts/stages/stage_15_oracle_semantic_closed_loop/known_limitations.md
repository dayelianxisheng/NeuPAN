# Known Limitations

- Oracle labels are simulation ground truth, not Stage 10 prediction.
- The bounded runs did not demonstrate goal-reaching navigation success.
- Semantic infeasible results and mixed-scene P2 results may be diagnostic-only when over the deadline.
- Static or instantaneous obstacles only; no future motion prediction.
- No formal safety guarantee is claimed.
- Depot is excluded from scored safety metrics because its vendor mesh is visual-only and license is unknown.
